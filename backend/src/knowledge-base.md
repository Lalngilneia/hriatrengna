## ABOUT HRIATRENGNA

Hriatrengna is a digital memorial and wedding album platform. It preserves legacies through beautiful online albums linked to unique QR codes. When someone scans the QR code on a plaque, grave marker, or wedding card, they are taken to the digital album.

The name "Hriatrengna" is a Mizo word meaning "remembrance" or "memory".

## MEMORIAL ALBUMS

### What is a memorial album?
A memorial album is a beautiful digital tribute to a loved one who has passed. It contains their photo, biography, dates, media (photos, videos, audio), tributes from family and friends, and a life events timeline.

### How do I create a memorial album?
1. Log in and go to your Dashboard
2. Click "Create Album"
3. Select "Memorial Album"
4. Enter the person's name, dates of birth and passing, and biography
5. Upload photos and videos
6. Click "Save & Publish"

### What can I add to a memorial album?
- Profile photo (avatar)
- Cover/banner photo
- Biography (rich text with formatting)
- Photos and videos
- Audio recordings
- Tributes (written messages from family/friends)
- Life Events Timeline (milestones like graduation, marriage, etc.)
- Background music

### How many photos can I upload?
This depends on your plan:
- Monthly: 200 photos, 10 videos
- Yearly: 500 photos, 30 videos
- Lifetime: 2,000 photos, 100 videos

### Can I password-protect my album?
Yes. In Album Settings → Security, you can enable password protection. Visitors will need the password to view the album.

### How do I publish my album?
In Album Settings, scroll to the bottom and click "Publish Album." Once published, anyone with the link or QR code can view it.

### Can I unpublish an album?
Yes. In Album Settings, click "Unpublish." The album will no longer be publicly accessible until you publish it again.

## WEDDING ALBUMS

### What is a wedding album?
A wedding album is a beautiful digital keepsake for couples. It contains the couple's photos, love story, wedding date, venue, and media from the wedding. Guests can scan a QR code at the venue and leave wishes.

### How do I create a wedding album?
1. Log in and go to your Dashboard
2. Click "Create Album"
3. Select "Wedding Album"
4. Enter the couple's names, wedding date, and venue
5. Upload photos and videos
6. Click "Save & Publish"

### What album labels are available?
- Pre-Wedding Shoot
- Ceremony
- Reception
- Honeymoon
- Anniversary

You can create multiple linked albums for one wedding (e.g., a pre-shoot album, a ceremony album, and a honeymoon album).

### How do guest wishes work?
Guests scan the QR code → visit your wedding album → tap "Wishes" tab → type their message and name. Their wish is submitted for your approval. You can approve, reject, or delete wishes in Album Settings → Wishes tab. Only approved wishes appear publicly.

### What wedding themes are available?
- Classic Romance — deep burgundy and gold, elegant serif fonts
- Floral Garden — soft pinks and illustrated borders
- Minimalist — black and white, clean modern design
- Royal — deep navy and gold, ornate style
- Retro Film — warm grain and vintage typography
- Tropical — coral, turquoise, and lush greens

### How do I change the theme?
Go to Album Settings → Theme tab → select any theme. The change takes effect immediately on the public page.

## QR CODES

### How do I get my QR code?
After publishing your album, click "Get QR Code" on your Dashboard. You can download the QR code as a PNG image.

### What is a QR Plaque?
A QR Plaque is a printable PDF or PNG with the album name, dates, QR code, and Hriatrengna branding in your chosen theme. It is designed to be printed and placed on a grave marker, memorial stone, or wedding card.

### How do I download the QR Plaque?
Go to Dashboard → Plaque → choose your theme → download PNG or PDF.

### Can I print the QR code?
Yes. Download the QR code PNG or the full plaque PDF and take it to any print shop.

## MEDIA & UPLOADS

### What file formats are supported?
- Photos: JPG, PNG, WEBP, HEIC (max 20MB each)
- Videos: MP4, MOV, AVI (max 500MB each)
- Audio: MP3, WAV, M4A (max 50MB each)

### Why is my upload failing?
Common reasons:
- File too large (check the size limits above)
- Unsupported file format
- Slow internet connection — try a smaller file first
- Storage quota reached — check your plan's photo/video limits

### Can I delete uploaded photos?
Yes. In Album Settings → Media tab, hover over any photo or video and click the delete button.

### Can I add captions to photos?
Yes. In Album Settings → Captions tab, you can add a caption to each photo.

## TROUBLESHOOTING

### The QR code is not working
- Make sure your album is published
- Check your subscription is active
- Try scanning with a different QR code scanner app
- Download a fresh copy of the QR code from your Dashboard

### I cannot log in
- Check your email and password
- Make sure your email is verified (check your inbox)
- Try "Forgot Password" to reset
- If you signed up with Google, use "Continue with Google"

### My album shows "Subscription Inactive"
Your subscription may have expired or payment failed. Check your subscription status in Account Settings. If you believe this is an error, contact support.

### Photos are not loading on the public album
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- If the issue persists, contact support

### I did not receive the verification email
- Check your spam/junk folder
- On the login page, click "Resend verification email"
- Make sure you entered the correct email address
